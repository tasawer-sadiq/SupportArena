## 💡 Quick Tips for Testing
1. Log in as **Citizen One** and report a critical threat (e.g. type `"electrical wire sparking over walkway"` in the title/description).
2. Note that the **Simulated Gemini AI Engine** immediately classifies it as **Critical** priority.
3. Sign out and click **Quick Access: Admin** to inspect the Admin Dashboard.
4. Verify the sparked wire report is shown at the top of the queue.
5. Click **Dispatch Organization**, select **Emergency Rescue Crew**, and input instructions.
6. Sign out and click **Quick Access: Emergency Rescue Crew** to inspect the organization dashboard.
7. Accept the task, post updates, and click **Mark Resolved** with feedback!
